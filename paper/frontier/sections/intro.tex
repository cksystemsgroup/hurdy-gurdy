\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:intro}

Present a platform any benchmark whose questions reduce to decision
procedures; the platform eventually learns all ways feasible in
practice to solve it, and saves, as structured evidence, everything
not yet solvable and why. The instrument that makes such a claim
checkable --- deterministic, fidelity-graded translation pairs with
untrusted authors --- exists and is measured
elsewhere~\citep{hurdygurdy2026calculus}; this paper states what it
is \emph{for}, and proves that it can do it. \Cref{sec:problem}
defines the problem; \Cref{sec:theorems} proves the six properties an
explorer needs; the sections between compress the instrument and its
loop to exactly what those theorems consume.
