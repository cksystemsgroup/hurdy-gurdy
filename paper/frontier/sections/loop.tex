\section{The loop}
\label{sec:loop}

The explorer runs two loops with different payoffs: a capability loop
triggered by unanswerable questions (the diagnosis names the missing
item) and a trust loop triggered by answers weaker than the asker's
floor (a second, independently derived route manufactures fidelity).
Unmet demand is recorded, aggregated per generation target, and
promoted to a registered design only by a human act --- or by a
human-written, revocable mandate whose mechanical instantiation is
delegated. Registered refinements of over-approximating pairs make
the cost axis a CEGAR loop whose refinements are reusable instruments
rather than solver-internal state.
