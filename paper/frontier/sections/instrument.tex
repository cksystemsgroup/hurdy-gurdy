\section{The instrument, as means}
\label{sec:instrument}

A trustworthy frontier map needs an instrument with specific
properties, and each is supplied by one feature of \hg: a map its
untrusted explorer cannot falsify needs determinism and the commuting
square; ``all ways'' needs routes, branches, and fidelity accounting;
the local gradient needs the five-obstacle diagnosis; ``everything
unsolvable, saved'' needs the books. This section states each feature
in one paragraph, as the requirement it serves.
