\section{Limitations and conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

\paragraph{Limitations}
This is a dated snapshot of a system designed to ratchet, and its
numbers say so: coverage is deepest on the spine (every accepted RV64IMC
probe surviving composed to SMT-LIB; the in-scope eBPF set complete) and
deliberately partial elsewhere (EVM at 86 of 144 opcodes; Wasm without
\texttt{loop}/\texttt{br}; Python an integer subset with bounded
unrolling) --- and the measured coverage is the \emph{acceptance} half
of \Cref{def:coverage}'s conjunction, over pair-owned probe inventories
(\S\ref{sec:eval-capability}). Universal answers are bounded claims
wherever BMC-style unrolling caps apply, and rest on
$\mathsf{universal}$-tier hypotheses that per-run evidence corroborates
but does not entail (\Cref{thm:universal}). The verified portion of the
C spine begins at the compiled binary: the compiler hop has no square
and contributes reproducibility plus a differential, not faithfulness
(\S\ref{sec:composition}). The \tprov\ tier's certified row is inhabited
with a \emph{formally verified} checker at the anchor
(\texttt{cake\_lpr}, \S\ref{sec:eval-proved}), but on a trivially unsat
instance; the residual \tcb\ is the bit-blaster's BV$\to$CNF step, and a
certified claim is per-question and bounded, never a proof of a pair.
Interpreter adequacy remains an assumption discharged empirically at
modest volume, and the diversity assumption behind branch corroboration
is structural, not probabilistic --- and scoped to the diverse prefix.
The compositional core of the calculus --- including the $n$-ary route
telescope --- is mechanized in Lean~4 with a clean axiom audit
(\S\ref{sec:mech}); the fieldwise loss computation, the retiming case,
and the specialization obligation of \Cref{thm:universal} remain
paper-stated.

\paragraph{Future work}
The gaps above are the roadmap: measure the true covered-and-faithful
conjunction over language-owned inventories; drive at least one route
with an external benchmark suite and a certified instance of nontrivial
size; estimate an escape rate for the gate (the blind-spot incident of
\S\ref{sec:bugs} proves it is nonzero); mechanize the specialization
obligation; and --- the missing half of the two-directional experiment
--- run and assess an LLM \emph{player} end to end.

\paragraph{Conclusion}
We proposed treating translations the way distributed systems treat
processes: as components that fail, whose failures are contained by
architecture --- declared projections, decidable squares, graded
evidence, independent routes, carried-back witnesses --- rather than
prevented by universal proof. The calculus makes the composition of
partial trust exact; the platform shows the discipline is buildable, by
agents, at measured (if modest) coverage; and the asymmetry theorems say
where proof effort actually buys assurance. The snapshot is the
baseline; the ratchet is the roadmap.
